MOIRANG LAKE TOURIST SPOT FLOATING LAKE

Loktak Lake, located near Moirang in Manipur, India, is the largest freshwater lake in Northeast India, famous for its unique phumdis—heterogeneous masses of vegetation, soil, and organic matter floating on its surface. It is home to the Keibul Lamjao National Park, the world's only floating national park, which serves as the last natural habitat for the endangered Sangai (dancing deer)..
