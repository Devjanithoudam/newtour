Essential Travel Tips

Prepare for your journey with these practical insights for an unforgettable Indian adventure.

Visa Requirements

Most travelers need an e-Visa. Apply online well in advance of your travel dates on the official Indian government portal.

Dress Modestly

When visiting temples and religious sites, ensure your shoulders and knees are covered. Carry a light scarf for convenience.

Stay Hydrated

Always drink bottled or filtered water, and avoid ice in drinks from street vendors to keep your trip trouble free.

Embrace the Chaos

India is vibrant, loud, and bustling. Approach the sensory overload with patience and an open mind for the most rewarding experience.
