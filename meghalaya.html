MEGHALAYA
Mystical, serene, and wrapped in drifting clouds, Meghalaya is a land where nature feels alive in every breath. With rolling hills, cascading waterfalls, and lush green valleys , it offers an escape into a world that is both tranquil and enchanting. From the living root bridges of Nongriat to the crystal-clear waters of Dawki River, every landscape feels almost unreal in its beauty. The mist-covered cliffs of Cherrapunji echo with the sound of waterfalls, creating a setting that is both dramatic and peaceful. Meghalaya invites you to slow down and reconnect—with nature, with silence, and with yourself. Walk through rain-kissed trails, witness clouds drifting beneath your feet, and experience a culture rooted in simplicity and warmth. Here, every moment feels fresh, pure, and quietly magical.
